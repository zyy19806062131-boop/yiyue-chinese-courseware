今天你的中文能量是多少？

先选择状态，再开始说。这个步骤让学生不用立刻进入“被考”的状态。

我和汉语的故事

我开始学汉语是因为……
Wǒ kāishǐ xué hànyǔ shì yīnwèi...
I started learning Chinese because...
一开始，我觉得……
Yī kāishǐ, wǒ juéde...
At first, I felt / thought...
后来，我发现……
Hòulái, wǒ fāxiàn...
Later, I found that...
对我来说，最难的是……
Duì wǒ láishuō, zuì nán de shì...
For me, the hardest part is...
以后，我希望……
Yǐhòu, wǒ xīwàng...
In the future, I hope to...
One real story is enough.先讲一件真实的小事。

我的好兄弟 / 姐妹

他 / 她是什么样的人？开朗、幽默、可靠、热心、安静、认真……
你们为什么关系好？认识很久、聊得来、互相支持、一起经历过很多事。
一句话
他是一个很可靠的人。
Tā shì yí ge hěn kěkào de rén.
He is a very reliable person.
一个原因
因为我有困难的时候，他会帮我。
Yīnwèi wǒ yǒu kùnnan de shíhou, tā huì bāng wǒ.
Because when I have difficulties, he helps me.
一个例子
比如，有一次我很难过，他听我说了很久。
Bǐrú, yǒu yí cì wǒ hěn nánguò, tā tīng wǒ shuō le hěn jiǔ.
For example, once I was very upset, and he listened to me for a long time.

我的梦想

slow speaking · no pressure
以前
我小时候想当……，因为那时候我觉得……
Wǒ xiǎoshíhou xiǎng dāng..., yīnwèi nà shíhou wǒ juéde...
When I was little, I wanted to be..., because at that time I thought...
现在
现在我的梦想变了，我更想……
Xiànzài wǒ de mèngxiǎng biàn le, wǒ gèng xiǎng...
Now my dream has changed. I want to... more.
原因
这个梦想对我很重要，因为它让我觉得生活更有方向。
Zhège mèngxiǎng duì wǒ hěn zhòngyào, yīnwèi tā ràng wǒ juéde shēnghuó gèng yǒu fāngxiàng.
This dream is very important to me because it makes me feel that life has more direction.
困难
最大的困难可能是时间不够，压力也会比较大。
Zuì dà de kùnnan kěnéng shì shíjiān bú gòu, yālì yě huì bǐjiào dà.
The biggest difficulty may be not having enough time, and the pressure may also be quite high.
计划
为了实现这个梦想，我需要先……，然后……
Wèile shíxiàn zhège mèngxiǎng, wǒ xūyào xiān..., ránhòu...
To achieve this dream, I need to first..., and then...

从短句到一小段

我的梦想是以后可以做一份自己喜欢的工作。这个梦想对我很重要，因为我希望生活更自由，也希望每天都能学习新的东西。为了实现它，我需要提高自己的能力，也需要更认真地计划时间。
Wǒ de mèngxiǎng shì yǐhòu kěyǐ zuò yí fèn zìjǐ xǐhuan de gōngzuò. Zhège mèngxiǎng duì wǒ hěn zhòngyào, yīnwèi wǒ xīwàng shēnghuó gèng zìyóu, yě xīwàng měitiān dōu néng xuéxí xīn de dōngxi. Wèile shíxiàn tā, wǒ xūyào tígāo zìjǐ de nénglì, yě xūyào gèng rènzhēn de jìhuà shíjiān.
My dream is to have a job I really like in the future. This dream is important to me because I hope life can be freer, and I hope I can learn something new every day. To achieve it, I need to improve my abilities and plan my time more carefully.
1
你小时候的梦想和现在一样吗？
Nǐ xiǎoshíhou de mèngxiǎng hé xiànzài yíyàng ma?
Is your childhood dream the same as your dream now?
2
为了实现这个梦想，你现在可以做一件什么小事？
Wèile shíxiàn zhège mèngxiǎng, nǐ xiànzài kěyǐ zuò yí jiàn shénme xiǎoshì?
To achieve this dream, what is one small thing you can do now?
3
如果失败了，你会怎么办？
Rúguǒ shībài le, nǐ huì zěnme bàn?
If you fail, what will you do?

我们的夜生活

你晚上的生活，更像哪一种？
Nǐ wǎnshàng de shēnghuó, gèng xiàng nǎ yì zhǒng?
Which type is your evening life more like?
我的夜生活比较简单。下课或者下班以后，我一般在家休息，有时候看视频，有时候学习中文。周末我可能会和朋友出去吃饭，但是我不太喜欢太吵的地方。
Wǒ de yè shēnghuó bǐjiào jiǎndān. Xiàkè huòzhě xiàbān yǐhòu, wǒ yìbān zài jiā xiūxi, yǒu shíhou kàn shìpín, yǒu shíhou xué zhōngwén. Zhōumò wǒ kěnéng huì hé péngyou chūqù chīfàn, dànshì wǒ bù tài xǐhuan tài chǎo de dìfang.
My nightlife is quite simple. After class or work, I usually rest at home. Sometimes I watch videos, and sometimes I study Chinese. On weekends I may go out for dinner with friends, but I don't really like places that are too noisy.
1
你喜欢安静的夜晚，还是热闹的夜晚？为什么？
Nǐ xǐhuan ānjìng de yèwǎn, háishi rènào de yèwǎn? Wèishénme?
Do you prefer quiet nights or lively nights? Why?
2
你的城市晚上有什么好玩的地方？
Nǐ de chéngshì wǎnshàng yǒu shénme hǎowán de dìfang?
What fun places are there at night in your city?
3
夜生活丰富，是好事还是坏事？这要看什么情况？
Yè shēnghuó fēngfù, shì hǎoshì háishi huàishì? Zhè yào kàn shénme qíngkuàng?
Is rich nightlife a good thing or a bad thing? What does it depend on?

网购的快乐与烦恼

你最近一次网购，买了什么？满意吗？
Nǐ zuìjìn yí cì wǎnggòu, mǎi le shénme? Mǎnyì ma?
What did you buy online most recently? Were you satisfied?

快乐

  • 不用出门，节省时间
  • 选择很多，价格可能更便宜
  • 可以看评价和买家照片
  • 很容易买到国外或小众商品

烦恼

  • 图片和实物不一样
  • 质量不一定好
  • 退货和等快递很麻烦
  • 容易冲动消费，买太多
你经常网购吗？
Nǐ jīngcháng wǎnggòu ma?
Do you often shop online?
我有时候网购，主要买衣服、书和生活用品。
Wǒ yǒu shíhou wǎnggòu, zhǔyào mǎi yīfu, shū hé shēnghuó yòngpǐn.
I sometimes shop online, mainly for clothes, books, and daily necessities.
你有没有买到过不满意的东西？
Nǐ yǒu méiyǒu mǎi dào guò bù mǎnyì de dōngxi?
Have you ever bought something you were not satisfied with?
有。有一次图片很好看，但是收到以后质量很差。后来我就会先看评价。
Yǒu. Yǒu yí cì túpiàn hěn hǎokàn, dànshì shōu dào yǐhòu zhìliàng hěn chà. Hòulái wǒ jiù huì xiān kàn píngjià.
Yes. Once the picture looked great, but the quality was poor when I received it. After that, I started checking reviews first.
我觉得网购有快乐，也有烦恼。最大的快乐是方便，因为不用出门就可以买很多东西。但是网购也有问题，比如质量不好、退货麻烦，或者容易买太多。所以我觉得网购很好，但是买以前要认真看评价，不要冲动消费。
Wǒ juéde wǎnggòu yǒu kuàilè, yě yǒu fánnǎo. Zuì dà de kuàilè shì fāngbiàn, yīnwèi bú yòng chūmén jiù kěyǐ mǎi hěn duō dōngxi. Dànshì wǎnggòu yě yǒu wèntí, bǐrú zhìliàng bù hǎo, tuìhuò máfan, huòzhě róngyì mǎi tài duō. Suǒyǐ wǒ juéde wǎnggòu hěn hǎo, dànshì mǎi yǐqián yào rènzhēn kàn píngjià, bú yào chōngdòng xiāofèi.
I think online shopping brings both happiness and trouble. The biggest happiness is convenience, because we can buy many things without going out. But online shopping also has problems, such as poor quality, troublesome returns, or buying too much easily. So I think online shopping is good, but we should check reviews carefully before buying and avoid impulse buying.
先说一次经历。一次真实网购，比空泛优缺点更自然。

如果没有手机

如果一天没有手机，你最不习惯的是什么？
Rúguǒ yì tiān méiyǒu shǒujī, nǐ zuì bù xíguàn de shì shénme?
If you had no phone for one day, what would you be most uncomfortable with?
早上 · 没有闹钟
出门 · 没有地图
买东西 · 不能支付
朋友 · 不能联系

观点展开

1
手机让生活更方便，比如可以联系朋友、查地图、付款和学习。
Shǒujī ràng shēnghuó gèng fāngbiàn, bǐrú kěyǐ liánxì péngyou, chá dìtú, fùkuǎn hé xuéxí.
Phones make life more convenient, for example, we can contact friends, check maps, pay, and study.
2
但是，手机也会让人分心，浪费时间，甚至影响面对面的交流。
Dànshì, shǒujī yě huì ràng rén fēnxīn, làngfèi shíjiān, shènzhì yǐngxiǎng miànduìmiàn de jiāoliú.
However, phones can also distract people, waste time, and even affect face-to-face communication.
3
所以我觉得手机很重要，但是我们不能太依赖它。
Suǒyǐ wǒ juéde shǒujī hěn zhòngyào, dànshì wǒmen bùnéng tài yīlài tā.
So I think phones are important, but we should not rely on them too much.
如果没有手机，我觉得生活会很不方便。因为我每天都用手机联系朋友、查地图、买东西和学习中文。但是，没有手机也可能让人更轻松。我们可能会少看短视频，多看书，多运动，也会更多地和身边的人聊天。所以我觉得，手机很好用，但是最重要的是找到平衡。
Rúguǒ méiyǒu shǒujī, wǒ juéde shēnghuó huì hěn bù fāngbiàn. Yīnwèi wǒ měitiān dōu yòng shǒujī liánxì péngyou, chá dìtú, mǎi dōngxi hé xué zhōngwén. Dànshì, méiyǒu shǒujī yě kěnéng ràng rén gèng qīngsōng. Wǒmen kěnéng huì shǎo kàn duǎn shìpín, duō kàn shū, duō yùndòng, yě huì gèng duō de hé shēnbiān de rén liáotiān. Suǒyǐ wǒ juéde, shǒujī hěn hǎo yòng, dànshì zuì zhòngyào de shì zhǎodào pínghéng.
If there were no phones, I think life would be very inconvenient. I use my phone every day to contact friends, check maps, buy things, and study Chinese. But not having a phone might also make people feel more relaxed. We might watch fewer short videos, read more, exercise more, and talk more with people around us. So I think phones are useful, but the most important thing is to find balance.
